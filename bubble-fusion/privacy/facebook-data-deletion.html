Facebook Account Binding Data Deletion Instructions

Thank you for playing Bubble Fusion! If you wish to delete all the personal data we have obtained from Facebook, please follow the instructions below.

1. Unbinding the Facebook Account

  1. Launch Bubble Fusion
  2. Go to the “Game Home” screen
  3. Open “Profile”
  4. Tap on “Account Binding”
  5. Select “Unbind Facebook Account”

After completing the above steps, we will immediately remove the login binding and delete all personal data obtained from Facebook.

Note: This action only removes all user information we obtained from Facebook, such as your Facebook name and email, and does not include any data within Bubble Fusion (e.g., game scores, level progress, etc.).

2. Effects After Deletion

  • Deletion Scope: Limited to all user information obtained from the Facebook account (e.g., Facebook name, email, etc.).
  • No Impact on Game Data: Your game records, level progress, and purchase history will remain in the game database and are not deleted when unbinding your Facebook account.
  • Re-binding: If you wish to link your Facebook account to Bubble Fusion again in the future, you can do so via the “Account Binding” feature within the game.

3. Further Inquiries and Contact

If you have any questions regarding account deletion, data privacy, or other game-related issues, please contact us by following the steps below:

  1. Launch Bubble Fusion
  2. Go to the “Game Home” screen
  3. Open “Profile”
  4. Tap on “Customer Service”
  5. Submit your inquiry, and we will get back to you as soon as possible

4. Privacy Policy and Terms of Service

For more information on how we handle personal data, please refer to our Privacy Policy . We are committed to protecting your personal information and complying with applicable laws and regulations.

Thank you for playing and supporting Bubble Fusion! If you have any questions, please feel free to contact us via our customer service channels.
